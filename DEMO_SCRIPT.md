## Recording Tips

### Tools
- **Screen Recording**: Loom, ScreenFlow, or OBS
- **Video Editing**: DaVinci Resolve (free), Final Cut Pro, or Adobe Premiere
- **Voiceover**: Blue Yeti mic or Rode NT-USB
- **Background Music**: Epidemic Sound, Artlist

### Technical Settings
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Frame Rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Audio: 44.1kHz, 16-bit minimum
- Export: MP4, H.264 codec

### Best Practices
- **Keep it short**: 2-3 minutes maximum
- **Show, don't tell**: More demos, less talking
- **Clear audio**: Use good mic, quiet room
- **Captions**: Add subtitles (80% watch muted)
- **Hook fast**: First 3 seconds matter most
- **CTA clear**: Tell them exactly what to do next
