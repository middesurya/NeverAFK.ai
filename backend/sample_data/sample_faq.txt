Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Course Access & Enrollment

Q: How do I access the course?
A: After purchasing, you'll receive an email with your login credentials. Log in at dashboard.example.com to access all course materials.

Q: How long do I have access?
A: You have lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates.

Q: Can I download the videos?
A: Yes! All video lectures are downloadable. Click the download icon below each video.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Not yet, but the website is fully mobile-responsive and works great on phones and tablets.

Technical Issues

Q: The video won't play. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
If issues persist, contact support@example.com

Q: I can't access the bonus materials.
A: Bonus materials are in the Resources section. Click "Resources" in the top menu, then "Bonus Materials."

Q: The practice exercises aren't loading.
A: Make sure you've completed the prerequisite lessons. Exercises unlock after completing the previous module.

Payment & Billing

Course Content

Q: Do I need prior experience?
A: No prior programming experience required! We start from the absolute basics.

Q: What software do I need?
A: You'll need:
- Python 3.10 or higher (free)
- A code editor like VS Code (free)
- About 2GB of free disk space

Q: How long does the course take?
A: Most students complete it in 6-8 weeks at 5-10 hours per week. But you can go at your own pace!

Q: Are there quizzes or tests?
A: Yes! Each module has practice exercises and a quiz. They're optional but highly recommended.

Q: What's covered in Module 3?
A: Module 3 covers Advanced Python Concepts including:
- Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)
- Decorators and generators
- Error handling and exceptions
- Working with files and databases
- Best practices and code organization

Certificates & Completion

Q: Do I get a certificate?
A: Yes! After completing all modules and passing the final project, you'll receive a certificate of completion.

Q: How do I download my certificate?
A: Go to Dashboard → Certificates. Click "Download" once you've completed all requirements.
