Course Module 1: Introduction to AI Support

Welcome to the AI Support course! In this module, you'll learn:

1. What is AI-powered customer support?
AI-powered support uses machine learning to automatically answer common questions and help customers faster.

2. Benefits of AI support:
- 24/7 availability
- Instant responses
- Reduced support costs
- Better customer satisfaction

3. How RAG works:
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) combines vector search with language models to provide accurate, context-aware responses based on your course materials.
